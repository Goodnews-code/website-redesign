Established in 1996, Joyful Heart Renewal Ministries is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization led by Dr. Ralph F. Wilson, Executive Director. From Loomis, California, Dr. Wilson has authored over 50 Bible studies and hundreds of articles.

The companion site JesusWalk.com features the comprehensive Bible study series, while JoyfulHeart.com offers articles, stories, and meditations. All contributions are tax-deductible.
