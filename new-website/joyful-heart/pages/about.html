About Dr. Ralph F. Wilson

Executive Director of Joyful Heart Renewal Ministries, pastor, author, and Bible teacher serving believers in 120+ countries since 1996.

Executive Director

Dr. Ralph F. Wilson

Dr. Ralph F. Wilson is the Executive Director of Joyful Heart Renewal Ministries, and writes nearly all the material on his various websites. He has an intense interest in using the Internet to teach the Bible and to train disciples.

Joyful Heart Renewal Ministries, Inc. is a California Religious Nonprofit Corporation formed "to encourage Bible study and discipleship among Christians, and to assist Christian laypeople, leaders, and pastors in their ministries." It is designated by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) as a 501(c)3 organization.Contributionsare tax-deductible.
