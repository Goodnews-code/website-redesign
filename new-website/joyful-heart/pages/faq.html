The main text in our Bible studies is the NIV, since Dr. Wilson's calling is to communicate the Word with clarity to the largest number of readers. However, in the numerous Hebrew and Greek word studies, he always refers to the KJV translation, as well as the NIV, the NASB, and, more recently, the ESV.

Some churches teach that the KJV is the only legitimate English translation (though this argument is becoming less common). However, as a student of the Bible for more than 70 years, Dr. Wilson has found that the ESV is to be preferred for word-for-word accuracy these days (and is similar in most readings to the NRSV). The NASB is a more literal translation than the ESV, but less readable. The NIV probably provides the best balance between readability and accuracy, hence its use in our Bible studies.More....
