JesusWalk: Beginning the Journey, new believer discipleship and spiritual formation curriculum

By Dr. Ralph F. Wilson

The 12-lesson series is coordinated withfree online lessons and videosor can be used with an optional book and optional DVD set. The printed book is available from Amazon and other booksellers.

Each lesson concludes with a memory verse, questions and discussion points, and an outline of the lesson. These provide the structure for a weekly meeting between a mentor and the new Christian or for use in a class or small group.
